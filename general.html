General Information

Participation

  • Participant needs to be a VU student and have proof of enrolment for verification
  • Participant needs to have a ESN card that is valid

Registration

  • Participant fills out the registration form .
  • Participant will submit their ESN card number at the moment of registration.
  • Participants can submit proof of enrollment at any point of the competition, however they cannot receive any prizes until enrollment has been verified.
  • Participant will get a free merch item of choice (as long as stock allows).
  • A designated spreadsheet will keep track of memberships.

Information required for registration

  • Full name
  • Email
  • Phone (optional, but enhances our ability to reach out to participants in the event they win)
  • Instagram (optional, but highly recommended so we can tag them)
  • Degree
  • Nationality
  • Date of birth
  • ESN card number
  • Accept data protection statement
  • Accept terms and conditions

After registration, participants will receive an email with their unique participation number.

Point system

  • Each activity will have a certain amount of points allocated that students can receive.
  • The amount of points that will be decided by the ESN board, and will be based on different factors such as difficulty, time invested, win/lose, etc.
  • There are participation points for all events, we will take attendance of each participant with their participant number.
  • In addition to the events, participants can get extra points at the end of each period if they got a grade equal to or higher than 8 (from 4th period onwards).
  • Participants will also get extra points if they apply to be the new ESN board!
  • There will be a 1st, 2nd and 3rd place, each with an assigned prize.
  • The prizes will be delivered to the winner when the contest has finished.
  • Winners will be announced in our social media and contacted by email/phone.
  • Case of tie in the end: raffle

To enter the contest, you must be a student of VU Amsterdam and a member of ESN VU Amsterdam.
