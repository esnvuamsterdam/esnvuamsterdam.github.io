FAQ

What are the prizes?

  • 1st place: New M1 Macbook Air - (worth €1230)
  • 2nd place: Bike - 1 year membership for Swapfiets (worth €250)
  • 3rd place: Giftcard of choice - €100

How will you choose a winner?

Winners will be chosen based on the number of points accumulated. If there is a tie, there will be a raffle to select a winner from the tied participants.

How can I know my score?

Feel free to send us an email at any time to find out your score.
Otherwise, stay tuned to the leaderboard and in our social media channels (instagram & facebook)

What gives the most points?

We have carefully spread the points so that you have many ways of collecting them.
Check our events and crazy 88 challenges.
