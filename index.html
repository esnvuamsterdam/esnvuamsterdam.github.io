Welcome to the ESN VU Amsterdam Spring Contest!

Looking to meet new people and make friends? Want to explore the beautiful city of Amsterdam? Wishing for a new high-end laptop, a new bike, or a gift card for your favorite store?

We have exciting news for you! ESN VU Amsterdam is organizing a fabulous, semester-long contest where you can experience all of the above and win incredible prizes, including a brand-new M1 Macbook Air, a Swapfiets bike, or a €100 gift card of choice!

The contest will include lots of fun events and challenges. The more events you participate in, and the more challenges you complete, the more points you can get! The three people who end up topping our leaderboard with the most points will receive one of the three prizes!

The contest starts on March 1st, so make sure to sign up with this form as soon as you get the chance! Sign-ups are possible at any point during the contest, so it’s fine if you’re late to the party. Just keep in mind that your co-contestants are racking up their point count!

To enter the contest, you must be an enrolled student of the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. This includes part-time and full-time Bachelor and Master students, as well as exchange students officially registered at the VU. You must also be a member of ESN VU Amsterdam. Please note that these requirements only apply if you want to participate in the contest and have a chance to win a prize. Otherwise, you can still join and participate in all of our events even without proof of enrollment and an ESN membership. Everyone is welcome!
