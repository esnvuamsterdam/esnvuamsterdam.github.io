Welcome!

Do you want a new laptop? Need a new bike? Or are you wishing for a gift card for your favorite store?
Well, we have exciting news for you! This semester, ESN VU Amsterdam is organizing a big semester-long contest! You can win incredible prizes, including a brand-new M1 Macbook Air, a Swapfiets bike, or a €100 gift card of choice!

The contest will include lots of fun events and challenges. The more events you participate in, the more points you can get! At the end of the contest, the three people with the most points will receive the awards.
Sign up with ⤑ this form quickly, as the contest starts on March 1st!

To enter the contest, you must be a student of VU Amsterdam and a member of ESN VU Amsterdam.
Sign up here to get an ESNcard.

If you want to find out more about the rules of the contest and the events, you can read our contest terms & conditions.

We hope to see a lot of you at our first event!
